## Calculations

1 -> 00, 256 -> ff

- 2 ^ 4 = 16 -> 0f
- 2 ^ 5 = 32 -> 1f
- 2 ^ 6 = 64 -> 3f
- 2 ^ 7 = 128 -> 7f
- 2 ^ 8 - 2 ^ 6 = 192 -> bf
- 2 ^ 8 - 2 ^ 5 = 224 -> df
- 2 ^ 8 - 2 ^ 4 = 240 -> ef
